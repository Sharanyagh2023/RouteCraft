# RouteCraft
AI-Powered Smart Multi-Modal Commute Optimizing System

**Overview**

An intelligent travel agent that eliminates app-switching fatigue by dynamically orchestrating the most cost-effective and efficient "split-journeys" between any two locations. Our AI engine analyzes real-time transport data and environmental factors to provide a single, optimized travel strategy tailored to commuter's current context. "Fastest", "cheaper" and "optimal" options are derived from the orchestrated data.




​⚡ Multi-Modal Split-Routes: Intelligently combines Auto, Metro, and Bus segments to save up to 75% in costs and 40% in time compared to direct cabs.

​📡 Real-Time Price Aggregator: Performs concurrent "bidding" across platforms like Uber, Ola, and Namma Yatri to pick the best value for every leg of your trip.

​🌦️ Weather-Aware Routing: Automatically filters out walking or open-air transit if rain is detected, prioritizing sheltered and safe alternatives.

​🔋 Low-Battery Safety Protocol: Detects device battery levels below 10% and instantly switches to the fastest direct route to ensure you reach home before your phone dies.

​🎯 One-Click Deep Linking: Seamlessly transitions from the RouteCraft interface to pre-filled booking screens on ride-hailing apps for a frictionless experience.
